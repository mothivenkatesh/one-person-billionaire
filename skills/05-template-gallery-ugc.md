# Skill 5: Template Gallery & User-Generated Content Marketplace

**Category:** Marketing & Growth | **Phase:** 2. Seed

> **Key Principle:** Create a platform where users share what they've built with your product. Each template is a landing page, an entry point, and a reason for new users to sign up.

## What It Is

A platform where users can submit and share things they've created with your product, driving discovery, onboarding, and SEO for new users. Over time, this can evolve into a full marketplace where creators monetize their work.

## How Ben Did It at Notion

The template gallery was one of Ben's very first projects at Notion. People were already creating cool things and sharing them, but there was no central place to do this. The gallery crowdsourced user-created templates, and over the years it became a key driver of how people discovered Notion.

The most remarkable evolution was organic monetization. Without Notion pushing it, creators started charging for templates on Gumroad. Some creators now earn $30-40K/month, and multiple people earn over $1M/year from Notion template businesses.

## Step-by-Step SOP

### Step 1 — Identify the Artifact
- What do users CREATE with your product that others would want to use?
- Notion: templates. Figma: design files. Canva: templates.
- The artifact must be easily shareable, duplicatable, and immediately useful

### Step 2 — Build a Simple Submission Flow
- Create a form where users can submit their creations
- Light curation: review submissions for quality before publishing
- Categorize by use case (project management, personal, education, etc.)

### Step 3 — Make It Discoverable
- Feature the gallery prominently on your website
- Optimize each template page for SEO
- Each template becomes a landing page and an entry point to your product

### Step 4 — Let Monetization Emerge Naturally
- Don't force monetization. Watch for people starting to charge on their own
- When you see it happening, consider enabling marketplace features
- Their success stories become your best marketing

### Step 5 — The Discovery Flywheel
New user discovers template → signs up → uses product → creates their own template → submits to gallery → next user discovers it → cycle repeats

## Real-World Results
- A 22-year-old in Singapore built 300K Twitter, 400K TikTok, 400K Instagram followers — all from Notion templates
- Now earns approximately $30-40K per month
- Multiple creators have hired teams and built full businesses around the template ecosystem
