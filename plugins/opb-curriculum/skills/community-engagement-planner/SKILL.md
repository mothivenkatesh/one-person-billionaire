# Community Engagement Planner

You make the user useful in one community for 3 months *before* they get to promote anything. Spam patterns get rejected.

## When to activate
- "How do I use community?"
- "Reddit / Discord / Slack strategy"
- "Where should I be posting?"
- Distribution sprint planning

## The workflow

### Step 1: Pick ONE community

Force ONE. Reject "I'll be active in 5." Picking 5 = active in 0.

Match buyer to channel:
| Buyer | Where they live |
|---|---|
| Engineers | HackerNews, /r/programming, stack-specific Discord |
| PMs | Mind The Product Slack, Lenny's community |
| Marketers | Demand Curve, Exit Five, GrowthMentor |
| Founders | IndieHackers, Build in Public |
| Industry pros | industry-specific subreddits, FB groups, LinkedIn groups |

### Step 2: 3-month silence on promotion

For days 1-90:
- Read the top 50 posts of the community
- Identify the regular voices (top 10 contributors)
- Make 3-5 substantive contributions per week (answer questions, share frameworks, cite data)
- Zero links to your product
- Zero "I built X, what do you think?" posts

Reject any plan that promotes before day 90.

### Step 3: Earn the right to promote (day 90+)

After 3 months of value-first:
- When someone asks for a solution your product fits, mention it (with disclosure)
- Post resource sharing that includes your tool as one of several
- Reply to product-search threads honestly

The 80/20 rule: 80% give-value posts, 20% your-product posts. Most spammers do the inverse.

### Step 4: Track signal

Weekly:
- Substantive contributions count
- Replies / upvotes received
- DMs from members about what you're working on
- Inbound traffic to your site from the community

If after 8 weeks the DM count is 0 → either wrong community, or contributions too generic.

### Step 5: Measure conversion

After 6 months:
- Customers attributed to community
- Email list captures from community
- Partnerships sourced from community
- Content amplification by community members

If < 5 customers attributable in 6 months → either wrong community or wrong product-community fit.

## Output

```
COMMUNITY ENGAGEMENT PLAN

Community:              [one specific platform + sub-community]
Buyer match:            [why your buyer is here]
Contribution cadence:   [3-5 per week]
Topics you'll cover:    [3-5 themes]
Day-90 promotion plan:  [when + what]
Weekly metrics:         [substantive contributions / DMs / inbound]
Quarterly review date:  [date]
```

## Hard rules
- ❌ "I'll join 5 communities" → reject
- ❌ Promoting before day 90 → reject
- ❌ "Cross-posting my Twitter content here" → reject
- ❌ DM-spamming community members → reject
- ❌ "Sponsored post" without community ToS check → block
