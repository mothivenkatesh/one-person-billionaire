# Guarantee Builder

*A 5-minute decision tool. Ends with a guarantee statement you can copy-paste into your pricing page.*

---

## Step 1: Pick your type

Answer 3 questions:

**Q1:** Is your cost-to-serve a refund < 10% of order value?
- ☐ Yes (small support cost, automatic refund possible)
- ☐ No (significant work involved)

**Q2:** Do you have ≥ 20 named testimonials with specific outcomes?
- ☐ Yes
- ☐ No

**Q3:** Can you measure the outcome you promise objectively?
- ☐ Yes (e.g., "saves X hours/week," "books N calls/month")
- ☐ No (subjective, like "feels productive")

| Q1 | Q2 | Q3 | Recommended type |
|---|---|---|---|
| Yes | No | No | **Unconditional** ("100% money back, 30 days") |
| Yes | Yes | Yes | **Outcome** ("Don't see X? We work free until you do") |
| No | No | Yes | **Conditional + Outcome combo** (default for AI products) |
| No | Yes | Yes | **Outcome** + **conditions** for filter |
| No | Yes | No | **Anti-guarantee** ("All sales final — here's why we're confident") |
| No | No | No | **Conditional** ("Money back if you complete steps X, Y, Z") |

---

## Step 2: Fill in the template

### If you picked Unconditional:

> **30-Day Unconditional Guarantee.** If you're not delighted with [product/service] for any reason within 30 days, email [email]. We'll refund 100% — no questions, no forms, no negotiation.

### If you picked Conditional:

> **30-Day Conditional Guarantee.** If you've completed all 3 setup steps and used [product] at least [N] times within 30 days and you're not satisfied, we'll refund 100%.

Conditions to consider (pick 1-3):
- ☐ Complete onboarding (define steps)
- ☐ Use the product N times within X days
- ☐ Implement the recommended workflow
- ☐ Provide written feedback on what's not working
- ☐ Other: ____________

### If you picked Outcome:

> **[Outcome] Guarantee.** If you don't [specific measurable outcome] within [timeframe], we'll [specific remedy: continue working free / refund + cash bonus / extend access].

Examples:
- "If you don't get to inbox zero by 9am within 30 days, we work free for an additional 30."
- "If your books aren't audit-ready by month 2, we refund the full setup fee + give you 3 months free."
- "If you don't book 3+ qualified sales calls in your first 30 days, full refund + we keep working free until you do."

### If you picked Anti-guarantee:

> **All Sales Final.** Here's why we don't offer refunds: [reasons rooted in proof — work is custom, prior client outcomes, etc.]. Before purchasing, please review [link to case studies] and book a [free strategy call] to confirm fit.

### If you picked Conditional + Outcome combo (recommended for AI products):

> **30-Day Outcome Guarantee.** If you don't [specific measurable outcome] within 30 days — *provided* you've completed the 3-step setup, used the product at least [N] times, and given us a chance to fix any blockers — we'll refund 100%. **And** if you DO see the outcome, we'll work free for an additional 30 days to entrench it.

---

## Step 3: Place it everywhere

Your guarantee is invisible if it's only in T&Cs. Place it:

- ☐ Hero section / above the fold ("Backed by our [name] Guarantee")
- ☐ Pricing page (full text + conditions)
- ☐ Order confirmation email
- ☐ Onboarding day 1 email (so customer knows the clock starts)
- ☐ Day 25 email ("You're 5 days from the guarantee window — let's confirm you're seeing [outcome]")
- ☐ Sales call deck
- ☐ Cold outbound message footer

---

## Step 4: Stress-test

Ask yourself:

- [ ] **Tire-kicker test:** Could a bad-fit buyer extract this without effort? If yes → tighten conditions.
- [ ] **Good-fit test:** Would a real customer feel the conditions are unreasonable? If yes → loosen conditions.
- [ ] **Specificity test:** Is "satisfied" vague? Replace with measurable outcome.
- [ ] **Trigger clarity:** Does the customer know exactly when/how to invoke?
- [ ] **Remedy clarity:** Does "make it right" have a specific meaning?

---

## Step 5: Math check

```
Net benefit = (Conversion lift % × ARPU) − (Refund rate % × ARPU × cost-to-serve multiplier)
```

For a $299/mo AI product:

| Refund rate | Conversion lift | Net benefit per customer |
|---|---|---|
| 5% | 20% | $44 |
| 10% | 30% | $60 |
| 15% | 40% | $77 |
| 5% | 0% | -$15 (don't bother) |

If conversion lift × ARPU > refund rate × ARPU × 1.5 → **offer the guarantee.**

For most AI products at $99-999 ACV with 5-10% refund rate: **always offer one.**
